Generate people

Submitting this form will generate JSON data about people.

Each person in the data will have the following attributes:

  • National Insurance Number
  • Family (last) name
  • Given (first) name
  • Date of birth (between 18 years old and 65 years old)
  • Job title
  • Favourite food
  • Car (consisting of type, make and model)

You can create any number of people. These people will be presented in a table on the next page for you to view.
